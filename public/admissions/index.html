Scholarships

Words Weave Magic at Shoolini LitFest

By awarding nearly 500 scholarships each year, Shoolini University recognises and rewards outstanding students. To meet the varied requirements of students, scholarships are divided into merit-based, need-based, and merit-cum-need categories. The scholarship sum varies depending on the recipient and can be anywhere from 10% to 100% of the tuition cost. The scholarship selection committee at the university carefully considers each application to make sure that deserving students get the scholarships they merit. Shoolini University offers chances for everyone to succeed, regardless of scholastic excellence or financial need.
