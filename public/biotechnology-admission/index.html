WHY SHOOLINI UNIVERSITY?

Shoolini University has an h-index of > 100 with a NAAC Grade. The University is UGC Approved & ISO Certified 9001:2008. We run Himachal Pradesh's first Government of India food testing laboratory and work as a nodal centre for institutions such as IIT Bombay, IIT Kharagpur and TCS.

We have evolved a unique pedagogy comprising an eUNIV for online access, a skills-based SPRINT program, involving writing seminars, open electives, flexible learning and a focus on liberal arts education.
