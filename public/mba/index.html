The Shoolini MBA Experience

Master of Business Administration (MBA) comes under the aegis of the School of Business Management at Shoolini University. Designed and run for future leaders of the corporate world, the Management School of Shoolini is ranked 102-125 among all Indian institutes at the 2022 NIRF Ranking. Students of MBA at Shoolini University are prepared to excel at a plethora of job profiles in the corporate world. The Management School is approved by HP PERC (Govt of HP) and is UGC approved.

Shoolini offers cutting-edge specialisations and a unique curriculum. Additionally, the MBA program at Shoolini University has seen a 100% placement record for its students. Theory, practical experiences, internships and campus placements have helped the university become a school of choice for its MBA programs. The National Board of Accreditation (NBA) has accredited the MBA Program at Shoolini University. With this, Shoolini becomes the first university in Himachal Pradesh to be granted NBA Accreditation for its MBA program.
