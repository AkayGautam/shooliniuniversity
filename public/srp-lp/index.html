The Faculty of Applied Sciences attracts some of the most talented students of India. Our vision is to develop technically qualified, skilled, and competent human resource through excellent teaching and hands-on training to cater to the talent requirements of the industry.
An integral part of the curriculum is training students to become future scientists. Students are trained on the molecular tools used to solve global issues. Additionally, students are encouraged to participate in innovation and startup workshops to promote entrepreneurship and research skills.

A RESEARCH-DRIVEN PROGRAM THAT PREPARES YOU FOR THE INDUSTRY

Join the bandwagon of leaders who learn from highly experienced, research-oriented postdoc faculty from top national, and international institutions. The program combines course work with research starting from first semester, preparing students for careers as professional biotechnologists, microbiologists, and future scientists

The degree follows a one-student one-patent norm that is supported by 2-+ high tech labs, a cancer research centre and several research fellowships for UG & PG students in government-funded projects.

With a focus on research and application-based curriculum, the BTech (Biotech) students of the Summit Research Program are trained to be scientists.
